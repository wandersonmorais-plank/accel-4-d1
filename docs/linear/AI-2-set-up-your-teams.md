# AI-2 — Set up your teams (Linear)

This document mirrors [Linear issue AI-2](https://linear.app/ai-accel-4/issue/AI-2/set-up-your-teams) so onboarding steps live next to the codebase.

## Workspace

This workspace is a container for your organization’s work.

- [Learn more about Workspaces](https://linear.app/docs/workspaces) — how to configure settings and workflows

## Teams

Teams are how you organize people and work in Linear.

- [Learn about Teams](https://linear.app/docs/teams) — how to structure teams and configure workflows

## Members

Teams are made of members with defined roles (Admin, Member, Guest).

- [Learn more about Members](https://linear.app/docs/invite-members) — add your team and assign roles

## Ready to add your team?

Invite via CSV or a unique link in [Linear member settings](https://linear.app/settings/members).
